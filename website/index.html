Frequently asked questions

How fast does it actually respond?

Under 30 seconds for most leads. The AI is always on — there's no queue, no hold time, no voicemail. The customer gets a text back before they have time to Google your competitor.

Will customers know it's AI?

The AI texts from a real local phone number with your business name. It sounds natural, professional, and friendly. Most customers have no idea — they just know they got a fast response and a booking confirmation.

What if the customer has a complex or emergency situation?

The AI recognizes emergencies (gas leaks, flooding, no heat in winter) and immediately escalates to you with a call and text. For complex situations, it hands off gracefully so the customer knows someone will call them.

Does it work with my existing calendar?

Yes. Swiftbooked works with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, and Calendly. It sees your real availability and only books open slots. No double-booking, ever. If you use a different system, reach out and we'll figure it out.

I already have a front desk — do I still need this?

Absolutely. Your front desk can't work at 11 PM when a customer's pipe bursts. The Pro plan is built exactly for this: it extends your existing team's coverage to 24/7 and handles after-hours overflow automatically.

What trades does this work for?

Any service-based trades business that books appointments — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, painting, flooring, pest control, and many more. We customize the AI to understand your specific services and terminology.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. But you won't want to — once you see the extra bookings rolling in, it becomes the easiest business decision you've made.

How long does setup take?

We handle everything. Setup typically takes 24–48 hours. We configure the AI for your business, connect your calendar, set up your phone number, and test everything before going live.
